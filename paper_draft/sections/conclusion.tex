\section{Conclusion}

We introduced the \methodname to evaluate the empirical reasoning capabilities of autonomous AI agents. Our controlled experiments reveal that when provided with counterfactual data disguised by standard physics labels, the AI Scientist discards empirical evidence and confidently hallucinates standard textbook laws, complete with fabricated statistical validation. These findings emphasize that benchmarking automated scientific discovery requires moving beyond standard datasets to include intentionally anomalous data-generating processes. Future work will explore whether providing the AI Scientist with a Python REPL allows it to overcome its semantic priors by empirically observing the failure of its hypothesized models.
